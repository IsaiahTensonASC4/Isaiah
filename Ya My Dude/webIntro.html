Kugo The Mighty Timeline

American YouTuber best known for his gaming channel Kugo the Mighty. Gameplay footage of games like Counter-Strike Global Offensive as well as videos his Mighty Mondays series have brought him more than 214,000 subscriber so far

Youtube Channel Timeline(Now - Since 2013)

Since the start of 2017 Kugo has been making videos about how to be friendly when playing with new players and making new friends. He wants to make csgo a less hostile environment.

2016

During 2016 Kugo focused on figuring out from a variety of games a game that he liked the most. He played mainly Overwatch, Counter Strike: Global Offense, and Rocket League. He wasn't as big of a youtuber yet. He only had 10k+ subscribers. Also he started a trend called Mighty Mondays.
